Hi, I’m Biswadeep Mazumder, a 21-year-old Computer Science undergraduate at Jorhat Engineering College who believes in becoming a jack of all trades, exploring everything from web development and web automation to machine learning and computer vision, while constantly sharpening my problem-solving mindset. My primary focus lies in Machine Learning and Deep Learning, where I’ve worked on multiple projects and continue to deepen my understanding every day.

I’ve completed two internships so far—one in research under IIIT Guwahati and one in industry at IOCL, Guwahati—which gave me a clear picture of how ideas move from academic theory to real-world production. I’ve also participated in multiple hackathons and even finished as a runner-up in one, which taught me how to build fast, work with a team, and think clearly under pressure.

When I’m not coding or training models, you’ll probably find me supporting Cristiano Ronaldo like it’s a full-time job, watching Real Madrid matches like my GPA depends on it, planning to sleep early but ending up with coffee at 2 AM, or overthinking conversations from 2019.
